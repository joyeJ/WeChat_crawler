Do you like to take selfies?

你们喜欢自拍吗？

If so, do you edit your selfies without being obvious about it?Or do you edit them with powerful filters and other photo editing tools?

如果喜欢的话，你们会有技巧性地把自己的照片修得很自然，还是会用超强的滤镜特效和其他修图工具狂修自拍照？

Be careful!

小心！


You wouldn't want your friends to stare at your pictures on Facebook or Instagram, thinking. . . "um… excuse me, who is she? ?"

你应该不希望你的好友盯着你在FB或IG上的照片想着「呃...小姐，你哪位啊？」

OK, so there's actually an interesting Chinese expression that describes such photos. It's called 'zhaopian'.

其实中文我们刚好有个有趣的说法，就是「照骗」。


Notice the 'pian' here means "trick or cheat." "Zhaopian" is a fairly new yet popular expression here in Taiwan, so a lot of my students have been dying to find out the English translation for it.

注意哦，其中的「骗」可是欺骗的「骗」哦。「照骗」在台湾是个蛮流行的说法，所以我的很多学生超~~~想要知道要如何把「照骗」翻成英文！

Well, a good translation would be "flattering photos." Here, flattering is used as an adjective that means making someone look or seem better or more attractive than usual.

嗯，我觉得英文有个不错的说法就是flattering photos，其中的flattering是个形容词，表示（照片等）比本人好看的。


So next time when someone says to you, "Wow, you look so nice and different in that picture," you could probably say something like, "Nah, it's just a flattering photo," or "I think the photo really flatters me." This will make you sound very modest.

所以下次当有人对你说："哇呜，你那张照片看起来很好看，好不同哦。"你就可以回"哎哦，这只是照骗啦！"或是"这张照片把我拍美了啦！"听起来会让对方觉得你很谦虚哦。


On the other hand, if you hear somebody says this to you, "This picture doesn't flatter you," don't get too upset. Hey, to be honest, I would actually take it as a compliment, cuz this just means you look much cuter in person.

另外，如果你听到有人这样对你说，"照片没有比你本人好看。"不要感到伤心哦。说真的，换做是我，我会觉得这是个称赞呢。因为这表示你本人好看多啰。




以后朋友圈评论，我又学会了一个高逼格的评论了，哈哈哈学到的词汇一定要用起来，这样才算是不浪费。
